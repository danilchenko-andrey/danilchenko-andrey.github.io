Privacy Policy for Just Checkers

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Just Checkers respects your privacy.

Just Checkers is a board game app for playing draughts/checkers. The app does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Game data

Game settings and progress may be stored locally on your device so the app can function properly. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Analytics and advertising

Just Checkers does not use third-party advertising SDKs.
Just Checkers does not use third-party analytics services to track you.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Children's privacy

Just Checkers does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

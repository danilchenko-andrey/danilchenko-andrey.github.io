Frequently Asked Questions

How do I undo a move?

Tap the undo button in the top-left corner of the screen. In single-player mode, undo reverses both your move and the AI's response.

How do I change AI difficulty?

Tap "New Game", then select a difficulty level: Easy, Normal, Hard, or Expert.

How do I play with a friend?

Tap "New Game" and choose "Multi-Player". Pass the device between turns — both players use the same screen.

Does the app need internet?

No. Just Checkers works entirely offline. No data is sent or received.

What are the rules of Russian checkers?

Russian checkers is played on an 8x8 board. Men move diagonally forward one square and capture by jumping over opponent's pieces. Kings ("flying kings") move any number of squares diagonally in any direction. Captures are mandatory. A man that reaches the last row is promoted to a king immediately, even mid-capture sequence.
